Adversarial Testing

THE SCENARIO LAB: PROVING ALGORITHMS WITH RIGOR

Case 1: Shortest Path vs. Optimal Cost

A heavy swamp water barrier (Cost = 10) is placed in the center; a clean flat bypass around it.

  • BFS: Plows directly through the lake to minimize hops.
  • Dijkstra: Detours intelligently around the flat grass to save cost.

Case 2: The DFS Ngõ Cụt Trap

A deep dead-end hallway is placed on the side; target is placed just 3 cells adjacent to start.

  • DFS: Dives straight into the dead end, checking hundreds of cells.
  • BFS: Spherical concentric search reaches target in 3 ticks.
